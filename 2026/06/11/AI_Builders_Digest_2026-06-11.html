Andrej Karpathy AI Researcher / Former Tesla AI

Andrej Karpathy calls Claude Fable 5 a "major-version-bump-deserving step change" — the same underlying model as Mythos but with added safeguards. Qualitatively it's a significant leap forward (on the order of Opus 4.5 in November), particularly for long problem-solving sessions on very difficult problems. The Jevon's paradox kicks in: his own demand for software is growing substantially as he can now ask for anything — explainers, visualizers, dashboards, bespoke single-use apps, 10X test suites. "Free your mind" — really looking forward to all the things people build.

Andrej Karpathy 称 Claude Fable 5 是"值得大版本迭代的阶跃式变化"——与 Mythos 相同的底层模型，但增加了安全护栏。他指出，从质量上看，这是一个显著的飞跃（堪比 11 月的 Opus 4.5），特别是对于极难问题的长时间解决会话。Jevon 悖论开始起作用：随着他现在可以要求任何东西——解释器、可视化工具、仪表板、定制的一次性应用、10 倍测试套件——他自己对软件的需求正在大幅增长。"解放你的思维"——非常期待人们会构建什么。

Alex Albert Anthropic Research Team

Alex Albert announces reset usage limits across Anthropic products and shares four tips for using Fable more effectively: (1) Give it bigger, more ambitious tasks. (2) Use xhigh/high effort as default. (3) Rework your skills and CLAUDE.mds — stale patterns from prior models anchor Fable to old behaviors. (4) Move from tasks to objectives — describe what done looks like and let Fable find the path. He also reflects that Fable made Claude feel less like a tool and more like something he collaborates with.

Alex Albert 宣布重置 Anthropic 产品的使用限制，并分享了四条更有效使用 Fable 的建议：（1）给它更大、更有雄心的任务。（2）将 xhigh/high effort 作为默认。（3）重新制作你的 skills 和 CLAUDE.md——来自之前模型的陈旧模式将 Fable 锚定在旧行为上。（4）从任务转向目标——描述完成是什么样子，让 Fable 找到路径。他还反思道，Fable 让 Claude 感觉不再像工具，而更像他协作的对象。
