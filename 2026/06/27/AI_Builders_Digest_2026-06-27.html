Cloudflare CEO: Bot Takeover, Edge AI & The Hard Decision Every CEO Will Face
The MAD Podcast with Matt Turck · 2026-06-25

Cloudflare CEO Matthew Prince explains that bot traffic has now surpassed human traffic on the internet in early 2026 — a structural inflection point. Over the next 5–10 years, internet traffic may increase 1,000×, fundamentally breaking the advertising-based business model because bots don't click ads.

The most notable decision Cloudflare has made recently: rightsizing its team to adapt to the AI era. Prince argues this is not a standard layoff — it's a recognition that AI changes the optimal team size for a given output. Companies that don't adapt their org design will be at a structural disadvantage.

Cloudflare's strategy: position itself as the "AI gateway" — the security, routing, and inference-serving layer between AI agents and the open internet. Cloudflare Workers is already processing millions of AI inference requests.

Cloudflare CEO Matthew Prince 表示，2026 年上半年机器人流量已首次超过人类流量——这是一个结构性拐点。未来 5–10 年，互联网流量可能增长 1000 倍，从根本上打破基于广告的商业模式，因为机器人不会点击广告。

Cloudflare 近期最引人关注的决定：调整团队规模以适应 AI 时代。Prince 认为这不是标准裁员，而是认识到 AI 改变了给定产出下的最优团队规模。不调整组织设计的公司将在结构性劣势中竞争。

Cloudflare 的战略：将自己定位为"AI 网关"——AI agent 与开放互联网之间的安全、路由和推理服务层。Cloudflare Workers 已在处理数百万次 AI 推理请求。

Claude Blog
Anthropic · Foundation Models Framework Support

Anthropic released Foundation Models framework support for Claude through a new Swift package, letting Apple developers use Apple's Foundation Models framework to call Claude for multi-step reasoning, code generation, and more. The framework returns typed Swift values through @Generable annotations.

What this unlocks: journaling apps can generate daily prompts on-device, then ask Claude to find threads across months of entries. One experience for the user, backed by the right model for each step.

Anthropic 通过新的 Swift 包发布了 Claude 对 Foundation Models 框架的支持，让 Apple 开发者可以使用 Apple 的 Foundation Models 框架调用 Claude 进行多步推理、代码生成等。该框架通过 @Generable 注解返回类型化的 Swift 值。

解锁的场景：日记应用可以在设备上生成每日提示，然后让 Claude 跨数月条目查找线索。对用户来说是一个无缝体验，每一步都由最合适的模型支撑。
