LIVE: Jensen Huang on Building the Dynamo of the Intelligence Age
TAKEAWAY Jensen Huang frames NVIDIA's role as the "dynamo of the intelligence age" — AI factories are the new electricity, and the infrastructure buildout underway is the largest in human history.

The AI factory thesis. Huang explains that most people still understand AI through the chatbot interface — you give it a prompt, it says something back. But the real shift is deeper: AI has evolved from text-to-text (ChatGPT) to anything-to-anything. Text to image, image to text, audio to video — the generative model is a universal translator between modalities.

Why AI factories are the best investment. An AI factory doesn't just answer questions — it generates intelligence as a product. Enterprises that build AI factories (the infrastructure to generate tokens at scale) will have a sustainable advantage. The "intelligence age" requires rethinking the entire computing stack: not just chips, but the system that turns data into intelligence.

The infrastructure buildout. Huang calls the current moment "the largest infrastructure buildout in human history" — bigger than even the industrial revolution. Every enterprise will need to become an "AI factory" to stay competitive. NVIDIA's position: they're not just selling chips; they're selling the dynamo that powers the intelligence age.

AI 工厂理论。 黄仁勋解释说，大多数人仍然通过聊天机器人界面理解 AI——你给它一个提示，它回复一些内容。但真正的转变更深刻：AI 已经从文本到文本（ChatGPT）演进到任何到任何。文本到图像、图像到文本、音频到视频——生成模型是模态之间的通用翻译器。

为什么 AI 工厂是最佳投资。 AI 工厂不仅仅回答问题——它生成智能作为产品。构建 AI 工厂（大规模生成 token 的基础设施）的企业将拥有可持续优势。"智能时代"需要重新思考整个计算堆栈：不仅仅是芯片，还有将数据转化为智能的系统。

基础设施大建设。 黄仁勋称当前时刻为"人类历史上最大的基础设施大建设"——甚至比工业革命还要大。每家企业都需要成为"AI 工厂"才能保持竞争力。NVIDIA 的定位：他们不仅仅是在销售芯片；他们销售的是驱动智能时代的发电机。
