Peter Yang Roblox PM

A sharp deconstruction of the "AI layoffs" narrative: whenever a CEO says "AI has changed how we work, so we must cut 1,000+ employees," Yang mentally translates it to "we overhired during the zero-interest era and need to cut costs. AI is easy to blame." Over 80,000 tech employees were laid off in Q1 alone, the highest since 2022-23. He offers 6 concrete strategies for employees to take back control rather than accept the framing.

一针见血地拆解了"AI 裁员"叙事：每当 CEO 说"AI 改变了我们的工作方式，所以必须裁掉 1000+ 人"，Yang 心里的翻译是"零利率时代过度招聘，现在要降本了。AI 是个方便的替罪羊。"仅 Q1 就有超过 8 万科技从业者被裁，是 2022-23 年以来最高。他给出了 6 条具体策略，帮员工夺回主动权，而不是被动接受这套叙事。

Nikunj Kothari FPV Ventures Partner

A contrarian frame on the state of VC: if you need a signal that venture capital has become consensus capital, this is it. The split he draws: founders who are "founders for the sake of being a founder" need capital to survive and will feel validated by consensus signals. But the truly obsessed founders treat each rejection as potential learning, not as a verdict on whether their company should exist. The implication: consensus-driven VC rewards conformity, not conviction.

对 VC 现状的逆向思考：如果你还需要一个信号来证明风险投资已经变成"共识资本"，这就是了。他画了一条分界线：那些"为了当创始人而当创始人"的人依赖资本续命，会被共识信号鼓舞。但真正痴迷的人把每次拒绝当作潜在的学习机会，而不是"你不该创业"的判决。含义很明确：共识驱动的 VC 奖励的是从众，不是信念。

Claude Blog Official Blog

Anthropic launches three major features for Claude Managed Agents. Dreaming is the headline: a scheduled process that reviews past agent sessions and memory stores, extracts patterns, and curates memories so agents self-improve over time. It can update memory automatically or let developers review changes before they land. Dreaming surfaces patterns a single agent can't see alone, including recurring mistakes, workflows agents converge on, and shared team preferences. Together, memory and dreaming form a two-part system: memory captures learnings during work; dreaming refines them between sessions. Outcomes lets developers write a rubric describing what "good" looks like; a separate grader evaluates output against criteria in its own context window, uninfluenced by the agent's reasoning. When something falls short, the grader pinpoints what needs to change and the agent takes another pass. Multiagent orchestration and webhooks are also now generally available, enabling agents to coordinate on complex tasks and integrate with external systems.

Anthropic 为 Claude Managed Agents 发布三项重大功能。Dreaming 是核心：一个定时流程，回顾 agent 的历史会话和记忆存储，提取模式并整理记忆，让 agent 随时间自我改进。它可以自动更新记忆，也可以让开发者先审核再落地。Dreaming 能发现单个 agent 无法独立察觉的模式，包括重复犯错、agent 趋同的工作流、以及团队共享的偏好。记忆和 Dreaming 组成双系统：记忆在工作时捕获学习成果，Dreaming 在会话间提炼优化。Outcomes 让开发者写一份"什么是好"的标准，独立的 grader 在自己的上下文窗口中评估输出，不受 agent 推理过程影响。不达标时，grader 精确定位问题，agent 再来一轮。Multiagent 编排和 webhooks 也已正式上线，支持多 agent 协作处理复杂任务并与外部系统集成。

Suno's Mikey Shulman: Everyone Can Make Music Now

The Takeaway Suno's real breakthrough is not AI-generated music but flipping consumers into creators: 90% of daily users create something, and the creation itself is the entertainment, not the output. The product insight is that music creation can be a mass-market leisure activity, not just a professional skill.

Mikey Shulman's path from Harvard physics PhD to Suno CEO is less improbable than it sounds. He credits his physics background with teaching him to operate at the nexus of two fields that don't usually interact, and that same mindset drives Suno: sitting at the intersection of AI, audio, and consumer entertainment.

The core product insight: before Suno, 8 billion people consumed music and very few created it. Now 90% of daily users create something. The counterintuitive part is that most people aren't creating to share or distribute; they're creating for the fulfillment of being creative. "The creation is actually the entertaining bit. And that is the big step change."

On the business model, Shulman draws a sharp distinction between Suno and the music industry's traditional framework. Suno is not trying to replace Spotify or record labels; it's building an entirely new category of creative entertainment where the act of making music is the product. The monetization follows from engagement, not from distribution rights.

On competition and moats: Shulman argues that the model is only a small piece of the value. The real moat is the product experience, the community, and the data flywheel from millions of users creating daily. He's skeptical of the "model layer is everything" narrative: "There's a reason why there's one Google and not ten, even though the search algorithm is published."

On the future: Suno is expanding beyond music into broader audio creativity. The vision is a platform where anyone can create any kind of audio content, from podcasts to soundscapes, with the same ease they currently create music.

Mikey Shulman 从哈佛物理博士到 Suno CEO 的路径，其实没那么离谱。他把物理学背景归功于教会自己"在两个通常不交叉的领域交界处操作"，同样的心态驱动了 Suno：坐在 AI、音频和消费者娱乐的交叉点上。

核心产品洞察：Suno 之前，80 亿人消费音乐，极少人创作。现在 90% 的日活用户会创作。反直觉的是，大多数人创作不是为了分享或传播，而是为了创作本身带来的满足感。"创作本身就是娱乐。这才是真正的范式转变。"

商业模式上，Shulman 划了一条清晰的界线：Suno 不是要取代 Spotify 或唱片公司，而是在构建一个全新的"创意娱乐"品类，制作音乐的行为本身就是产品。变现逻辑来自参与度，而非分发权。

关于竞争和护城河：Shulman 认为模型只是价值的一小部分。真正的护城河是产品体验、社区、以及数百万用户每日创作形成的数据飞轮。他对"模型层决定一切"的叙事持怀疑态度："搜索算法都发表了，但 Google 只有一个，不是十个。"

关于未来：Suno 正从音乐扩展到更广泛的音频创作。愿景是一个任何人都能以同样的便捷度创作任何音频内容的平台，从播客到声景。
