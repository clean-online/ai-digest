Aaron Levie Box CEO

Two posts on the future of AI deployment. The long one argues that AI is fundamentally different from software — models change so much they can meaningfully alter workflows with each upgrade, so the "deliver stable tech and they adopt it" playbook breaks down. The logical conclusion: Forward Deployed Engineering (FDE) becomes a core competency at scale, because one vendor sharing best practices across thousands of companies is more efficient than every company learning them alone. Also: "Headless software is the future" — the interface layer detaches from the intelligence layer.

两条关于 AI 部署未来的推文。长推论证 AI 和软件有本质区别——模型变化大到每次升级都可能显著改变工作流，"交付稳定技术、客户采纳"的打法失效了。逻辑结论：Forward Deployed Engineering（FDE）在大规模部署时成为核心竞争力，因为一个供应商跨数千家公司分享最佳实践，比每家公司自己摸索高效得多。另外："无头软件是未来"——界面层与智能层正在分离。

Unsupervised Learning (Redpoint AI)

Ep 86: Yann LeCun on Leaving Meta, Breaking The LLM Paradigm, & Why Hinton is Wrong

The Takeaway Yann LeCun is leaving Meta to start his own company, AMI — not because of conflict, but because the LLM paradigm has hit its ceiling. He argues that autoregressive text prediction can never achieve true intelligence; the future lies in JEPA (Joint Embedding Predictive Architecture), which learns world models through observation, not language. His divergence from Hinton and Bengio is not academic — it's architectural.

Yann LeCun, one of the three Turing Award winners and a founding father of deep learning, is spinning out from Meta to launch AMI. He clarifies the biggest misconception: his departure wasn't about conflict with Mark Zuckerberg or Alex, but about a fundamental disagreement on research direction. At Meta, the organizational momentum had shifted toward scaling LLMs, while LeCun believes that path is fundamentally limited.

His core thesis: autoregressive LLMs can never achieve true intelligence because they only model the surface statistics of language, not the underlying world. They have no understanding of physics, no persistent memory of experiences, and no ability to plan. He calls the current LLM paradigm "a dead end for intelligence" — useful for tools, insufficient for agents.

The alternative: JEPA (Joint Embedding Predictive Architecture), which learns internal world models by predicting abstract representations of observations, not pixel-level or token-level reconstructions. LeCun argues this is how biological intelligence works — babies learn physics before language. JEPA-style systems could build persistent world models, reason about cause and effect, and plan multi-step actions without language as a crutch.

On his divergence from Hinton and Bengio: LeCun says their views started splitting in 2023. Hinton believes scaling current architectures with more data and compute will eventually produce intelligence. LeCun disagrees — "more of the same won't get you to something fundamentally different." He's equally skeptical of the AI safety doom narrative, calling it "science fiction" that distracts from real near-term risks like bias and job displacement.

On Meta's FAIR legacy: he's proud of what the lab built — PyTorch, the Llama models, fundamental research in self-supervised learning — but the company's priorities shifted toward product-driven LLM scaling, leaving less room for the kind of blue-sky research he wants to pursue.

The most provocative line: "Five years, complete world domination." When asked about the path of progress, LeCun half-jokes that the best way to get breakthrough research is to "hire the best people and get the fuck out of the way." AMI will be his vehicle to prove that JEPA can do what LLMs can't.

Yann LeCun，深度学习三巨头之一、图灵奖得主，正在从 Meta 离职创办 AMI。他澄清了最大的误解：离职不是因为与扎克伯格或 Alex 的冲突，而是对研究方向的根本分歧。在 Meta，组织势能已经转向扩展 LLM，而 LeCun 认为这条路有根本性局限。

核心论点：自回归 LLM 永远无法实现真正的智能，因为它们只建模语言的表面统计规律，而非底层世界。它们不理解物理，没有持久的体验记忆，也没有规划能力。他称当前 LLM 范式是"智能的死胡同"——作为工具有用，作为 Agent 不够。

替代方案：JEPA（联合嵌入预测架构），通过预测观测的抽象表示（而非像素级或 token 级重建）来学习内部世界模型。LeCun 认为这正是生物智能的工作方式——婴儿先学物理，再学语言。JEPA 类系统能构建持久的世界模型，推理因果关系，并规划多步行动，无需依赖语言。

关于与 Hinton 和 Bengio 的分歧：LeCun 说他们的观点从 2023 年开始分化。Hinton 认为用更多数据和算力扩展当前架构终将产生智能。LeCun 不同意——"更多同样的东西不会让你得到根本不同的东西。"他同样对 AI 安全末日论持怀疑态度，称其为"科幻"，分散了对偏见和失业等真实近期风险的注意力。

关于 Meta FAIR 的遗产：他为实验室的成果自豪——PyTorch、Llama 模型、自监督学习基础研究——但公司重心已转向产品驱动的 LLM 扩展，给他想追求的蓝天研究留下的空间越来越少。

最挑衅的一句话："五年，完全的世界统治。"当被问及进步路径时，LeCun 半开玩笑说，获得突破性研究的最好方式是"雇最好的人，然后滚蛋让他们干活"。AMI 将是他证明 JEPA 能做到 LLM 做不到之事的载体。
