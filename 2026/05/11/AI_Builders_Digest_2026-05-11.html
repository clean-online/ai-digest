Anthropic Engineering Official Blog

An honest post-mortem on three separate changes that caused Claude Code quality degradation in April 2026. Issue 1 (March 4): switched default reasoning effort from high to medium — wrong tradeoff, reverted April 7. Issue 2 (March 26): a bug cleared thinking every turn instead of once per idle session — made Claude appear forgetful, fixed April 10. Issue 3 (April 16): added a verbosity-reducing system prompt that hurt coding quality — reverted April 20. The key lesson: approval fatigue is the real risk, and when three changes hit different traffic slices on different schedules, aggregate effects look like broad degradation even when each change was small.

一篇坦诚的事后分析，解释今年四月导致 Claude Code 质量下降的三次独立变更。第一（3月4日）：将默认推理精力从高切换到中——错误的权衡，4月7日回滚。第二（3月26日）：一个 bug 导致每次对话都清除思考内容而非仅在空闲会话后一次——让 Claude 显得健忘，4月10日修复。第三（4月16日）：添加了降低冗长的 system prompt，损害了编码质量——4月20日回滚。关键教训：审批疲劳才是真正的风险，当三次变更在不同时间表影响不同的流量切片时，聚合效应看起来像广泛的质量下降，即使每次变更都很小。

ElevenLabs' Mati Staniszewski: How Voice Becomes the Interface for Everything

The Takeaway ElevenLabs was born from a childhood frustration watching foreign films dubbed in Poland with a single monotone narrator. Mati Staniszewski and cofounder Piotr built a company on the thesis that everyone deserves to speak any language with the same emotion and intonation. The real insight: voice is the most emotionally loaded data channel humans have, and the companies that figure out how to move at the speed of imagination — not the speed of production — will own the next interface layer.

Mati Staniszewski's ElevenLabs story starts not in 2022 but in high school in a Warsaw suburb — where he and his cofounder Piotr became best friends, studied together, traveled together, and eventually worked together. The founding inspiration: watching any foreign movie in Polish and hearing it dubbed by one single monotone narrator, forcing viewers to "interpret your own emotions for that content." The thesis: everyone should be able to speak any language with the same emotion and intonation.

The conversation digs into what makes ElevenLabs different in a crowded voice AI market. The company wasn't chasing the TTS quality wars — it was building for a specific use case that nobody else was addressing: dubbing, localization, and cross-lingual emotional transfer. The insight that lands: voice is the most emotionally loaded data channel humans have. When you strip the emotional signature from a voice, you're stripping the human signal. ElevenLabs figured out that the real unlock wasn't better TTS — it was voice identity preservation across languages.

On the product side, Mati explains how the interface of the future will be voice — not just for commands but for creative expression, emotional communication, and immersive experiences. The key quote: "We're building for the day when you can have a real conversation with any character, in any voice, across any language — and it feels completely natural." The business model is expanding from B2C AI speech tools to B2B localization infrastructure — studios, publishers, and game companies are ElevenLabs' fastest-growing segment.

The counterintuitive insight: the hardest part of voice AI isn't technical — it's the uncanny valley of emotional authenticity. Users can forgive imperfect pronunciation; they cannot forgive a voice that sounds emotionally wrong. That's why ElevenLabs' real moat is not the model architecture but the emotional validation data they've accumulated — millions of human ratings of what makes a voice feel "right."

Mati Staniszewski 和 ElevenLabs 的故事不是从 2022 年开始的，而是从华沙郊区的高中时代开始的——他和联合创始人 Piotr 成了最好的朋友，一起学习、旅行，最终一起工作。创业灵感：看任何外语电影时，波兰语配音只有一个单调的叙述者在说话，强迫观众"自己去想象内容的情感"。 thesis：每个人都应该能够用相同的情感和语调说任何语言。

对话深入探讨了 ElevenLabs 在拥挤的语音 AI 市场中的差异化。不是在追求 TTS 质量之争——而是在构建一个没有其他人在解决的具体用例：配音、本地化和跨语言情感迁移。落地的关键洞察：语音是人类最具情感负载的数据通道。当你去掉声音的情感特征时，也去掉了人类信号。ElevenLabs 真正的突破不是更好的 TTS——而是跨语言的语音身份保持。

在产品方面，Mati 解释了未来的界面将如何通过语音实现——不仅仅是命令，还有创造性表达、情感交流和沉浸式体验。关键语录："我们正在构建的那一天，你可以与任何角色进行真正的对话，用任何声音，在任何语言中——而且感觉完全自然。"商业模式正在从 B2C AI 语音工具扩展到B2B 本地化基础设施——工作室、出版商和游戏公司是 ElevenLabs 增长最快的细分市场。

反直觉的洞察：语音 AI 最难的部分不是技术——而是情感真实性的恐怖谷。用户可以原谅不完美的发音；他们无法原谅情感上"不对"的声音。这就是为什么 ElevenLabs 真正的护城河不是模型架构，而是他们积累的情感验证数据——数百万个人类评级，判断什么让声音"感觉对"。
