Training Data
From SEO to Agent-Led Growth: ProFound's James Cadwallader
The Takeaway AI is creating an entirely new marketing discipline — Agent-Led Growth — where your brand's survival depends on how you show up when AI agents research, compare, and recommend on behalf of users. This is not SEO 2.0; it's a fundamentally different game with new rules, new winners, and new losers.

James Cadwallader, co-founder and CEO of ProFound, joins Training Data to discuss what may be the biggest platform shift in marketing history: the move from blue-link search to probabilistic AI responses.

"The big misconception with using agents to build marketing is that it's just a way to automate the work that we've been doing in the past," Cadwallader explains. "The reality is quite different. You can now, because of agents and because of LLMs, do a type of marketing that just frankly was not possible before."

ProFound helps companies understand how they show up in AI search — when ChatGPT, Claude, and other AI systems research products and services on behalf of users. With ChatGPT rolling out ads and every marketer scrambling to understand generative engine rankings, this is suddenly relevant to every business.

The core insight: in the old SEO world, you optimized for human attention. In the new AI agent world, you're optimizing for AI reasoning — what facts does the model cite, what comparisons does it make, what recommendations does it surface? The inputs that determine these outputs are fundamentally different from keywords and backlinks.

Cadwallader's advice for marketers: stop thinking of this as technical SEO and start thinking of it as model relations — building the kind of authoritative, well-structured presence that AI systems trust and cite. It's not about tricking the algorithm; it's about becoming genuinely值得信赖 by it.

ProFound 联合创始人兼 CEO James Cadwallader 登上 Training Data，探讨营销史上可能是最大的一次平台迁移：从蓝链搜索到概率性 AI 回答。

"用 AI agent 做营销的最大误解，是以为这只是把我们过去做的事情自动化了，" Cadwallader 解释道。"现实完全不同。因为有了 agent、有了 LLM，你现在可以做一种以前根本不可能的营销。"

ProFound 帮助企业了解自己在 AI 搜索中的表现——当 ChatGPT、Claude 和其他 AI 系统代表用户研究产品和服务时。随着 ChatGPT 推出广告、每个营销人都在拼命搞懂生成式搜索引擎的排名规则，这件事突然变得与每个企业都相关了。

核心洞察：在旧的 SEO 世界里，你的优化目标是人类注意力。在新的 AI agent 世界里，你在优化的是 AI 的推理——模型会引用哪些事实？做哪些比较？推荐哪些结果？决定这些输出的输入，和关键词、外链完全不是一回事。

Cadwallader 给营销人的建议：别再把这当成技术性 SEO，开始把它当成模型关系管理——建立那种 AI 系统信任并引用你的、权威且结构良好的存在感。不是去骗算法，是让它真正觉得你是值得信赖的。
