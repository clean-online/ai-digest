Building a School Where AI Models Learn About Humanity
TAKEAWAYSurge 把自己定位为「AGI 的学校」——模型像孩子一样到来、离开时更聪明。它卖的差异化不是规模，而是品味与专家判断。这个隐喻把数据/eval 工作重构成了「育人」，而非简单训练。
Edwin, founder & CEO of Surge, describes his company as building "a school for AGI" — a place where AI models come to learn about humanity and how to run the world. Models arrive unformed, like children, and leave smarter, more creative, and more thoughtful. The curriculum shifts as they mature — what you teach in preschool differs from college, and "it's not just that they're more advanced." Surge provides data environments and evals for model companies, with an unusual emphasis on taste and expert judgment in data, and famously reached roughly $1B in revenue without raising outside money. Edwin frames their work as "raising AGI" — a distinct way of thinking about data as pedagogy rather than mere optimization.
Edwin，Surge 创始人兼 CEO，把公司描述为在建造「一所 AGI 的学校」——AI 模型来这里学习人性、学习如何运转世界。模型像孩子一样未成形地到来，离开时更聪明、更有创造力、更深思熟虑。课程随模型成熟而演变——你在幼儿园教的和大学教的不同，「而且不只是更高级」。Surge 为模型公司提供数据环境和 eval，罕见地强调数据中的品味与专家判断，并 famously 在不拿外部融资的情况下做到约 10 亿美元营收。Edwin 把这份工作称为「raising AGI」——一种把数据当作育人、而非单纯优化的独特思路。

Claude Code now supports artifacts
Starting today, Claude Code can capture work progress as an artifact — turning its work into live, shareable visual pages (PR walkthroughs, system explainers, dashboards, release checklists) that update themselves as the session runs. It's built on the full context of your session — your codebase, connectors, and the conversation itself — so a single incident page can fuse the failing test, the error spike from a monitoring tool, and the root-cause reasoning from the session. No data sources to wire up, no infra to stand up: you ask for a page, and Claude Code builds it from what's already there.
从今天起，Claude Code 可以把工作进度捕获为artifact——把它的工作变成实时、可分享的视觉页面（PR 走查、系统讲解、数据看板、发布清单），并随会话进行自我更新。它建立在你会话的完整上下文之上——你的代码库、连接器，以及对话本身——所以一个事故页面就能把失败的测试、监控工具里的错误峰值，以及本次会话的根因推理融合在一起。无需接数据源、无需搭基建：你要一个页面，Claude Code 就用已有的东西把它建出来。
