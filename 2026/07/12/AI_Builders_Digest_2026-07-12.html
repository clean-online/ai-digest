The MAD Podcast with Matt Turck

Stripe's AI Chief: How AI Agents Will Buy, Sell, and Pay

The Takeaway Agentic commerce has gone from hypothetical to real infrastructure in a year — but the binding constraint is trust, not capability. Stripe's answer: a standardized protocol (ACP) plus a wallet where you set the guardrails.

Matt Turck sits down with Emily Sense, Head of Data & AI at Stripe, to go deep on the economic stack for AI agents. Her headline: agentic commerce has shifted in a year from hypothetical to real, deployed infrastructure — with actual companies building on it and repeatable patterns to learn from.

Stripe's Agentic Commerce Protocol (ACP, co-built with OpenAI) standardizes how businesses expose catalogs, inventory, and prices to agents, and passes a shared, tokenized, platform- and PSP-agnostic payment token so the agent never holds raw credentials. It's already adopted by Best Buy, Coach, URBN, Kate Spade, Wix, Shopify, and BigCommerce, and integrates with Google/Gemini, Microsoft, OpenAI, and Meta.

Stripe frames autonomy in levels (L1 human-led execution → L3 fully autonomous, like self-driving). Consumers sit around L2 today — leaning on AI to find products but still choosing the exact item. The most under-discussed risk, per Emily: token theft — she cites more than one in six signups at AI companies as this kind of abuse.

Link Wallet (built on Stripe's 300M-user consumer wallet) lets users set budgets and guardrails and revoke agent access at any time — a more flexible successor to the original one-time-use virtual cards. The larger thesis: agents are already lifting business formation (5M US solopreneurs earning a living; hundreds of thousands clearing $1M/yr), and long-term, agent-to-agent transactions could strip friction from B2B markets (Coase's theory of the firm).

Matt Turck 对话 Stripe 数据与 AI 负责人 Emily Sense，深入拆解 AI agent 的经济技术栈。她的核心判断：代理式商务（agentic commerce）一年内从概念变为真实、已部署的基础设施——有真实公司在上面构建，也有可复用的模式可供学习。

Stripe 的 Agentic Commerce Protocol（ACP，与 OpenAI 共建）把“企业向 agent 暴露商品目录 / 库存 / 价格”和“共享、令牌化、跨平台且跨支付处理商（PSP）的支付令牌”标准化，agent 永不持有原始凭据。Best Buy、Coach、URBN、Kate Spade、Wix、Shopify、BigCommerce 已采用，并与 Google/Gemini、Microsoft、OpenAI、Meta 打通。

Stripe 用自主度分级描述（L1 人主导执行 → L3 全自主，类比自动驾驶）。今天消费者约在 L2——依赖 AI 找货但仍自己选定商品。Emily 认为最被低估的风险是令牌窃取：她指出 AI 公司超过 1/6 的注册是这类滥用。

Link Wallet（基于 Stripe 3 亿用户的消费钱包）让用户设定预算与护栏、随时收回 agent 权限——是初代一次性虚拟卡的更灵活继任者。更宏观的论点：agent 已在拉动创业（美国 500 万单人公司靠此维生、数十万年收入破百万），长期看 agent 对 agent 的交易可消除 B2B 市场摩擦（科斯的“企业理论”）。
