Aaron Levie Box CEO

Levie expands on Cursor's new research into multi-model agentic systems. The key pattern: a frontier model acts as planner/orchestrator while cheaper workhorse models handle execution, yielding a 15X cost improvement. Only a few moments in a complex task — original decomposition, design decisions, key trade-offs — actually require frontier intelligence. Once the planner collapses ambiguity into explicit instructions, cheaper models just follow. This routing architecture, Levie argues, is where the applied layer will differentiate: companies that master multi-tier model routing across domains (coding, finance, legal, healthcare) will unlock larger workloads that were previously too expensive.

Levie 深入解读 Cursor 多模型 agentic 系统的新研究。核心模式：一个前沿模型当规划器/编排器，更便宜的「主力」模型做执行，实现 15 倍成本下降。复杂任务中只有少数时刻——初始分解、设计决策、关键取舍——真正需要前沿智能。一旦规划器把模糊性压成明确指令，便宜模型只管执行。Levie 认为这种路由架构是应用层差异化的关键：能跨领域（编程、金融、法律、医疗）掌握多层级模型路由的公司，将撬动之前因成本过高而无法承接的大批量工作负载。

No Priors
Travel Through the Lens of AI with with Booking.com CEO Glenn Fogel
Takeaway There is no moat — only continuous innovation. The companies that build real value will endure, just as they did through the dot-com era.
No Priors hosts Booking Holdings CEO Glenn Fogel, a 27-year veteran who joined Priceline in 2000 when it was worth a few hundred million and helped grow it to a $180B travel giant.

The Takeaway: There is no moat — only continuous innovation. "The only way to win long term is to continue to develop new services, new ways to do things." The AI era, like the dot-com era, will see massive speculative excess, but the companies that build real value will endure.

Key insights:

1. Dot-com lessons for AI. Fogel sees direct parallels between the late-90s internet boom and today's AI wave — same speculative frenzy, same massive capital flows, same likelihood that the vast majority of companies won't survive. But the ones that do will be transformative. Priceline went from a $6 reverse-split stock to nearly $6,000 — a 1,000x return over 25 years.

2. Penny: Booking's agentic travel assistant. Adoption is doubling every month, delivering higher conversion, faster search, shorter path to booking, lower cancellation, and higher customer satisfaction. But Fogel is cautious — at $186B in annual travel volume, Penny is still tiny in absolute terms. The real questions are unit economics (token cost per trip, ROI, lifetime value impact) and model routing (which model for which task at what cost).

3. AI in customer service. Cost per contact is already down ~10%, and customer satisfaction is up — AI resolves issues faster than humans. But Fogel warns against over-automation: some customers genuinely want to talk to a human, and the business must recognize that boundary.

4. The $700M reinvestment calculus. Booking is investing ~$700M this year in tech enablement, including AI, with savings from operational efficiency. Fogel's capital allocation framework: invest in the company if ROI justifies it → acquire if better opportunities exist → return to shareholders if neither works. "That's what I've always believed in."

5. The AI hype cycle and democracy. Fogel notes that polling data shows people love AI when asked about specific benefits (helping kids with school, making life easier) but fear it when asked about job destruction — suggesting the framing of the question drives the answer. In a democracy, politicians will exploit both narratives for votes, making honest public discourse about AI's real impact difficult.

核心观点

没有护城河——只有持续创新。真正创造价值的公司会留下来，就像互联网泡沫时代一样。

No Priors 对话 Booking Holdings CEO Glenn Fogel，一位在 Priceline 服务 27 年的老兵——2000 年加入时公司市值仅数亿美元，一路带到 1800 亿美元的旅行巨头。

核心观点：没有护城河——只有持续创新。「长期赢的唯一方法是不断开发新服务、新做法。」AI 时代如同互联网泡沫时代，会经历大规模投机性过剩，但真正创造价值的公司会留下来。

关键洞察：

1. 互联网泡沫给 AI 的教训。Fogel 认为 90 年代末的互联网热潮与当下 AI 浪潮有直接可比性——同样的投机狂热、同样的巨额资本涌入、同样大概率多数公司活不下来。但活下来的那些将改变世界。Priceline 从合股后 $6 涨到近 $6000——25 年 1000 倍回报。

2. Penny：Booking 的 agentic 旅行助手。采用量每月翻倍，带来更高的转化率、更快的搜索、更短的预订路径、更低的取消率和更高的客户满意度。但 Fogel 很谨慎——在 1860 亿美元年交易量面前，Penny 的绝对值还很小。真正的问题是单位经济学（每次行程的 token 成本、ROI、终身价值影响）和模型路由（什么时候用哪个模型、花多少钱）。

3. AI 客服。每次联系成本已下降约 10%，客户满意度上升——AI 比人类更快解决问题。但 Fogel 警告不���过度自动化：有些客户确实想和人说话，企业必须识别这个边界。

4. 7 亿美元再投资算账。Booking 今年投入约 7 亿美元用于技术赋能（含 AI），资金来自运营效率节约。Fogel 的资本配置框架：公司投资 ROI 够好就投 → 并购机会更好就买 → 都不行就把钱还给股东。「这是我一直信奉的。」

5. AI 炒作周期与民主。Fogel 指出民调数据很矛盾：当问及具体好处（帮孩子做功课、让生活更便捷）时人们爱 AI，当问及失业风险时人们怕 AI——问题的框法决定了答案。在民主制度下，政客会利用两种叙事拉选票，这使得关于 AI 真实影响的诚实公共讨论变得困难。
